3. DENTAL HEALTH TIPS PAGE (/beratung/zahngesundheit-tipps)
   Purpose: Educational content about dental care

   Quick Stats Bar:
   - 2x täglich putzen (brush twice daily)
   - 2min pro Putzvorgang (2 minutes per brushing)
   - 1x täglich Zahnseide (daily floss)
   - 2x Zahnarzt pro Jahr (dentist twice yearly)

   Six Tip Categories:

   1. Putztechnik (Brushing Technique) - Blue
      - Brush 2x daily for 2 minutes
      - Use gentle circular motions
      - Angle brush 45° to gum line
      - Don't forget inner surfaces and chewing surfaces
      - Brush your tongue

   2. Ernährung (Nutrition) - Green
      - Reduce sugary snacks and drinks
      - Eat calcium-rich foods (milk, cheese)
      - Drink water instead of soft drinks
      - Enjoy sweets right after main meals
      - Chew sugar-free gum for saliva production

   3. Fluorid (Fluoride) - Purple
      - Use fluoridated toothpaste (1450 ppm)
      - Additionally use fluoride mouthwash
      - Spit out toothpaste but don't rinse immediately
      - For children: use age-appropriate fluoride toothpaste
      - Ask dentist about fluoride varnish

   4. Zahnzwischenräume (Interdental Spaces) - Pink
      - Use dental floss daily
      - Alternative: interdental brushes for larger spaces
      - Guide floss gently in C-shape
      - Clean each space individually
      - Use water flossers as supplement

   5. Zahnarztbesuche (Dental Visits) - Orange
      - Visit dentist at least 2x yearly
      - Get professional cleanings
      - Speak openly about complaints
      - Use early detection examinations
      - Schedule appointments in advance

   6. Zahnfleischpflege (Gum Care) - Red
      - Watch for bleeding gums as warning sign
      - Gently massage your gums
      - Use special gum care products
      - Avoid aggressive brushing
      - For inflammation: see dentist immediately

   Daily Routines:

   Morning Routine:
   1. Breakfast (healthy, tooth-friendly)
   2. Wait 30 minutes (for acidic foods)
   3. Brush teeth (2 minutes with fluoride toothpaste)
   4. Mouthwash (optional, for extra protection)

   Evening Routine:
   1. Use dental floss (clean all interdental spaces)
   2. Brush teeth (2 minutes, all surfaces)
   3. Mouthwash (for overnight protection)
   4. No more eating or drinking (except water)

   FAQ Section:
   Q: Wie lange sollte ich wirklich Zähne putzen?
   A: At least 2 minutes, most people only brush 45-70 seconds

   Q: Ist elektrische Zahnbürste besser als manuelle?
   A: Can remove up to 21% more plaque, but technique is most important

   Q: Wann sollte ich die Zahnbürste wechseln?
   A: Every 3 months or when bristles are frayed, after illness

   Q: Ist Zahnseide wirklich notwendig?
   A: Yes! Toothbrush only reaches 60% of tooth surfaces

ANIMATION SYSTEM
----------------

Animation Classes:
- will-animate: Prepares element for animation
- animate-slide-in-left: Slides content from left
- animate-slide-in-right: Slides content from right
- animate-fade-in: Fades in content
- animate-fade-in-up: Fades in while moving up
- scroll-animate: Triggers on scroll
- hover-lift: Lifts element on hover
- hover-scale: Scales element on hover
- gradient-animate: Animates gradient backgrounds

Timing:
- Base transition: 300ms
- Timing function: cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)
- Delays: Staggered animations use 100-300ms delays

Intersection Observer:
- Threshold: 0.1
- Root margin: 0px 0px -100px 0px (triggers before fully visible)
